ABOUT US

Founded in 2022, the minds of Adriel Hunter and Michael Milano wanted to challenge what a cross-media company is.

In creating products for a multitude of different mediums, our fans are our top priority. We believe that creating relationships with our community can result in some terrific bonds, inspire us to create, and help us further push our creativity to new heights. We strive to create a relaxed yet diligent studio environment with an amazing workflow. We employ and aim to hire the best in the business. Our independent studio are looking for passionate game creators to join our accomplished team and make our visions a reality.
